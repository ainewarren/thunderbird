What is Intuitive Counselling?

Thunderbird helps people work through all manner of issues that are negatively affecting their mental/emotional health and preventing them from having the quality of life that they deserve.

He uses a combination of his clinical skills & spiritual gifts (empath, clairaudience, clairsentience) to:

  • see and feel into the original source of the wound;
  • clear and heal the energy at the spiritual level;
  • identify and clear self-defeating patterns and coping strategies and instill healthy ones;
  • and empower the client to move forward using their own strengths and spiritual gifts.

About Tristan MacKenzie

Thunderbird (aka Tristan MacKenzie) is a natural born healer and spiritual intuitive who is certified and insured to practice shamanic energy healing and intuitive counselling in the UK and abroad.

His counselling qualifications include: a Masters degree in Human Relations Counselling, post-Masters coursework and clinical therapeutic counselling, and a Masters degree in Theology and Ethics. His shamanic training includes: Masters level coursework in Native American Studies and training/certification for energy healing with a Native American shaman who has "reminded him" of the ways and means of shamanic practice that he already knows.

Thunderbird specialises in grief, trauma (including PTSD), heartbreak, men's mental health, and LGBT+ healing from minority stress/oppression.
